Pumpkin Cake with Cake Mix

Pumpkin cake with cake mix is a quick and easy dessert made by combining canned pumpkin with a boxed cake mix. The result is a moist, spiced cake with rich pumpkin flavor.

Often topped with cream cheese frosting or a dusting of powdered sugar, this cake is perfect for fall. Variations may include nuts, chocolate chips, or warm spices for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 cup pumpkin
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean, about 45 minutes.
