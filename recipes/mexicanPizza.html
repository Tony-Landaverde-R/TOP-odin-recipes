Mexican Pizza Recipe

Description

Mexican pizza is a crispy tortilla layered with refried beans, seasoned beef, melted cheese, and topped with tomatoes, onions, and jalapeños. Instead of pizza sauce, it's often finished with tangy salsa or enchilada sauce.

Popular in homemade and fast-food versions, it offers a crunchy, savory bite. Some variations add black olives, sour cream, or guacamole for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • ¼ cup sour cream (Optional)
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced jalapeño pepper
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup salsa
  • 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Cook the beef with onions and garlic, drain, and season.
  2. Place a tortilla on each pie plate and top with refried beans.
  3. Cover with the beef, top with another tortilla, and bake until the tortillas are crisp.
  4. Layer the toppings in this order: Salsa, cheeses, tomatoes, green onions, and jalapeño.
  5. Bake until the cheese is melted. Cool, then cut each pizza into four slices.
